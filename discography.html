Introduction

Queen discography consists of studio, live and compilation albums. In this page, studio albums will be shown, with their main information. Regardless of the fact that most songs were composed by Freddie Mercury, all Queen's member influence strongly contributed to forge the Queen legendary sound. Click here to check Queen's main page.
